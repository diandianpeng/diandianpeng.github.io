Research Interests

  • Developing and applying novel numerical-modeling techniques.
  • The interplay between mantle dynamics and tectonic plates.
  • The diverse range of subduction processes and their mechanisms.
  • The formation, evolution, and dynamics of LLSVPs and mantle plumes.
  • Plume–slab interactions and the resulting lithospheric deformation and volcanism.
  • Using geophysical, geochemical, and geological data to constrain mantle dynamics and lithospheric structure.

Computational & Technical Skills

  • Geodynamic modeling: global and regional mantle-convection modeling with sequential data assimilation; forward and adjoint (retrodiction) approaches linking deep-mantle flow to surface observables.
  • Modeling & visualization tools: CitcomS, ASPECT (mantle convection); GPlates (plate reconstructions); ParaView, VisIt, GMT.
  • Programming: Python (NumPy, SciPy, Pandas, Matplotlib), C, C++, Fortran, MATLAB, Bash; Git, Linux.
  • Methods & computing: finite-element and finite-difference methods, parallel / high-performance computing (TACC Frontera), inverse problems, signal processing, and machine learning.
